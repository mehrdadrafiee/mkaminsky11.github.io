  • September 2013

    Science Fair Project Started

    With the help of Eben Cross, Research Scientist at MIT, my brother and I started to build a project to enter into the Massachusetts State Science and Engineering Fair. Our goal of our project was to find the corellation between weather patterns and air pollution. To do this, I bought a Raspberry Pi, and attached an AirPi module. The code for the AirPi was written in Python, and I modified it to fit our needs (specifically, so that it recorded the data onto a file). The Raspberry Pi was then launched via weather balloon to collect data. The project was ultimately successful, and advanced past the regional level to the state fair.
